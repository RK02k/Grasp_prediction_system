"""
Default prompt used by the VLM to infer grasp intent from a segmented object.
"""

Given the image of an object highlighted with a green mask, analyse and answer:

1. What object is the robot likely trying to grasp?
2. What is the most probable grasp intent (e.g., power grasp, precision pinch, lateral grasp)?
3. Which part of the object should the robot's fingers contact?
4. Are there any affordances or constraints that affect the grasp strategy?

Provide a concise, structured answer.
